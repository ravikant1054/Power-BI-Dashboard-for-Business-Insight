Sales & Logistics Performance Dashboard (Power BI)
============================================
Project Overview
-------------------------
This project is a multi-layered Power BI dashboard designed to provide a 360-degree view of business operations. It integrates Sales Performance, Logistics & Delivery, and Customer Experience metrics to help stakeholders identify growth opportunities and operational bottlenecks.

The analysis covers data from 2022 to 2023, showcasing year-over-year growth and regional performance across various Indian states.

 Key Dashboard Sections
The report is structured into six interactive pages:

Sales Performance: Tracks monthly sales trends, total revenue (₹9M), and identifies top-tier customers.

Logistics & Delivery: Monitors supply chain efficiency, focusing on delivery delays by state and average processing times.

Customer Experience: Analyzes feedback sentiment (Positive vs. Missing) and maps customer satisfaction across regions.

Executive Summary: A high-level KPI cockpit showing Total Sales (₹93.96 Lakhs), Unique Customers (860), and Average Quantity (10.45).

Business Insights: Data-driven narratives explaining the "Why" behind the numbers.

Strategic Suggestions: Actionable recommendations to improve ROI and operational speed.

 Key Insights & Findings
Revenue Growth: Total sales reached ₹93.9 Lakhs. Performance improved from ₹4.5M in 2022 to ₹4.9M in 2023, showing healthy YoY growth.

Regional Leaders: Kerala is the top-performing state (~₹1.6M sales) but also faces the highest delivery delays (116 cases).

Operational Efficiency: The average order processing time is 4.88 days.

Customer Sentiment: Out of 775 total reviews, only 325 are positive, indicating a significant opportunity to improve service quality.

Seasonality: Strong sales peaks are observed around March and early 2023, suggesting seasonal demand fluctuations.

Technical Stack
Power BI Desktop: For report authoring and data visualization.

Power Query: Used for data cleaning, transformation, and ETL processes.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Created custom measures for KPIs like Average Quantity, Total Sales, and Review Percentages.

Data Modeling: Established relationships between Sales, Customer, and Geography tables.

Strategic Recommendations
Logistics Optimization: Target high-delay states like Kerala and Punjab to optimize warehouse and courier partnerships.

Customer Retention: Implement loyalty programs for high-performing states and faster resolution protocols for negative feedback.

Market Expansion: Run targeted promotions in Rajasthan, Gujarat, and Karnataka to boost their currently lower revenue contributions.

Inventory Management: Increase stock levels during peak months (March/April) to meet seasonal demand efficiently.
